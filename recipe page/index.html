Grandma's Secret Apple pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apple

Instructions


  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a bowl
  3. Top with cinnamon sugar mixture
